The next ice age

might start in another 50.000 years

It's not easy to predict when the next ice age will begin, but it's estimated to occur in approximately 50.000 years. When I refer to an ice age I mean a glacial period, a time when substantial portions of Earth are covered in ice. Currently we are technically in an ice age, specifically in an interglacial period. However, the term ice age can have varying interpretations depending on who you ask.

Anyway in the next ice age, I envision Earth resembling the icy landscapes of Star Wars' Hoth. I think I might prefer the warmer conditions of a Greenhouse Earth.

If we take 50.000 years as a given and assume an average human lifespan of 80 years, the next ice age is projected to occur in approximately 625 lifetimes.
