might start in another 50.000 years

It's not that easy to predict when the next ice age will start. What I mean with ice age is a glacial period: a period in which substantial parts of Earth are covered in ice. Currently we are technically in an ice age, in an interglacial period to be more precise. But even though ice age is commonly used, its meaning depends on who you'll ask.

Anyway in the next ice age, I visualize Earth to be in a Star Wars' Hoth like landscape. I think I might prefer the Greenhouse Earth (read: the warmer periods).

If I'd take 50.000 years as a fact and I assume our lives last for 80 years, then the next ice age will happen in another 625 lifetimes.
